ITM 295R

Project-Based Learning Experience

This course emphasizes hands-on project development and real-world application of course concepts through individual and collaborative work.

Course Description and Learning Outcomes (CLOs)

Course Description

This project-focused course provides students with opportunities to apply theoretical knowledge through practical, hands-on projects. Students will develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and project management skills while working on real-world challenges in their field of study.

Course Learning Outcomes (CLOs)

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

  • CLO 1: Identify and analyze complex problems within the field and propose innovative solutions
  • CLO 2: Design and execute comprehensive project proposals with clear objectives and methodologies
  • CLO 3: Apply project management principles to complete projects within specified timelines and constraints
  • CLO 4: Communicate project findings effectively through written reports and oral presentations
  • CLO 5: Collaborate effectively with team members and stakeholders throughout the project lifecycle
  • CLO 6: Evaluate project outcomes and reflect on lessons learned for continuous improvement
